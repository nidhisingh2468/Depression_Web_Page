What is
DEPRESSION?

Depression is more than just a temporary feeling of sadness; it's a real and complex medical condition. It can affect anyone, regardless of age, gender, or background. Depression can impact your daily life, relationships, and overall well-being.

How Can We Help?

Our Depression Detection App is designed to provide you with support and guidance on your journey to better mental health. We offer:

  • Depression Screening: Take our quick and confidential self-assessment to determine if you may be experiencing symptoms of depression.
  • Resources: Access a wealth of information, articles, and resources to help you better understand depression and its treatment.
  • Community Support: Connect with others who are facing similar challenges and share your experiences in a supportive and safe community.
  • Professional Guidance: Seek guidance from mental health professionals and find the help you need to overcome depression.
